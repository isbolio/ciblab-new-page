Jorge Luis Dávila Guerrero

Professor & Ph.D. Student

Jorge Dávila is currently working as a Professor in the Department of Engineering at Universidad de Guadalajara, CUCSUR, and he is a Ph.D. student at the Computer Intelligence CUCEI. His research topic is entitled “Application of Artificial Intelligence models for the analysis of meteorological variables in agricultural environments” in which, by means of machine learning models, he aims to reduce the dimensionality of the variables that directly influence the estimation of evapotranspiration.
