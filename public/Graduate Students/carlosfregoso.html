Carlos Alberto Fregoso Iturria

Biomedical Engineer & Graduate Student

Carlos Alberto Fregoso Iturria graduated in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Guadalajara and is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Applied Computing at the same institution. His main research focuses on the classification and prediction of hormonal pathologies, particularly Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), using supervised neural network algorithms. Additionally, Carlos is involved in various research projects related to biomedical technology and aims to develop tools for the early detection and prevention of diseases, supporting better healthcare outcomes.
