Welcome to the Computer Intelligence and Biorobotics Lab

About Us

The Computer Intelligence and Biorobotics Lab (CIBLab) is a laboratory dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence and its application in critical sectors such as medicine, biology, agronomy, robotics, industrial processes, and energy.

Our Mission

Through the design and implementation of advanced machine learning techniques and metaheuristic methods, the laboratory addresses complex problems, promoting innovative and sustainable solutions.

What We Do

  • Develop algorithms and computational tools for automation in monitoring and quantification.
  • Model and simulate complex problems using artificial intelligence.
  • Innovate solutions for critical sectors such as healthcare, energy, and robotics.
